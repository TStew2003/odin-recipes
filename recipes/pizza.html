Pizza Recipe

Delicious Pizza Recipe Description

A pizza is a flat, open-faced baked pie of Italian origin, consisting of a thin layer of bread dough topped with spiced tomato sauce and cheese, often garnished with anchovies, sausage slices, mushrooms, etc.

I personally am a fan of pizza but that is because I'm a picky eater. Pizza is usually loved by everyone and is essential when going to an Italian restaurant. I am very much looking forward to going to a nice Italian place for some soon.

Ingredients Needed:

  • Various meats
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Vegetables of any kind
  • Pizza dough

Steps:

  1. Start with crust.
  2. Add a sauce.
  3. Add some veggies if you'd like.
  4. Add some protein if you'd like.
  5. Add cheese on top.
  6. Bake your creation in a hot ovenat 450F or above.
