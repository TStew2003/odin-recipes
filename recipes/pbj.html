PB&J Recipe

Delicious PB&J Recipe Description

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a wonderful creation. It consists of two pieces of bread followed by peanut butter and jelly in between them.

I personally am a fan of PB&J but that is because I'm a picky eater. PB&J is usually loved by everyone and is essential when going to any restaurant. I am very much looking forward to going to a nice place for some soon.

Ingredients Needed:

  • Two slices of bread of your choice.
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Steps:

  1. Lay down two slices of bread.
  2. Put peanut butter on both slices.
  3. Put jelly on one slice.
  4. Put the two slices of bread together.
  5. Slice them in half.
  6. Enjoy.
